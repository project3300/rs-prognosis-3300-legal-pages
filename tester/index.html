Become a tester

If you want to help validate upcoming builds, send your email address and device type. The signup goes straight to the Pocket AI Mechanic tester workflow so support can review it without relying on drafted email.

Tester signup is limited to the information needed to contact you and match you to Android or iOS test builds.

The site sends this data to the Pocket AI Mechanic signup workflow. If submission is unavailable, you can still use the direct support email link below.

What to expect

Testers get access to the latest features to help validate real world results. It costs nothing to become a tester and you have free access to the application.

What support receives

  • Your email address.
  • Your selected device type.
If you later need to share logs, screenshots, or vehicle details, you can do that in direct follow-up rather than on the public signup page.
