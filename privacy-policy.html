Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pocket AI Mechanic ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and stores information when you use the Pocket AI Mechanic mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account information from sign-in providers, such as user ID, name, email, and profile image when granted.
  • App data you create, including vehicle details and diagnosis records.
  • Technical data needed to operate the app, such as local settings and secure authentication tokens.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your signed-in session.
  • Save and synchronize vehicles and diagnosis data between sessions or devices.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve app features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service providers required to operate sign-in, subscriptions, hosting, analytics, or platform services.

Data Retention

We retain account-linked data while your account is active or until deletion is requested, subject to legitimate operational or legal requirements.

Your Choices

  • You can use the app without signing in where local-only mode is available.
  • You can request deletion of account-related data using our published deletion process.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored data. No storage or transmission method is guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For privacy requests or questions, email support@pocketaimechanic.com and include "Privacy Request" in the subject line.
