User Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how to request deletion of account-related data for RS Prognosis 3300.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to project3300@users.noreply.github.com.
  2. Use subject: Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include the sign-in email or provider user ID used in the app.

What Gets Deleted

  • Account profile records stored by our backend (where applicable).
  • Associated cloud-stored vehicle and diagnosis data tied to the account.

What May Remain

  • Minimal technical logs retained temporarily for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.
  • Local data on your own device until you uninstall/clear app data.

Timeline

We aim to process requests within 30 days.

Confirmation

We will confirm once deletion is complete (or if additional verification is needed).
