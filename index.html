FAQ

Questions drivers ask before they trust the diagnosis.

Clear answers help position the product as serious, capable, and useful for both beginners and experienced enthusiasts.

How does the AI diagnosis work?

The app analyses the problem description, available vehicle context, and supporting evidence to rank likely causes and recommend sensible next checks.

Can it use photos?

Yes. Photos can add context such as warning lights, leaks, damaged parts, labels, or VIN information that would otherwise be hard to describe accurately.

Can it analyse sounds?

Yes. Engine noise and other unusual sounds can be captured and used as part of the issue analysis flow.

Does it work with OBD devices?

Pocket AI Mechanic is designed to work with compatible Bluetooth OBD hardware so code, VIN, and live data context can strengthen the diagnosis.

Is it suitable for beginners?

Yes. The workflow is built to make the next step clearer, whether you are an experienced enthusiast or just trying to understand a fault before visiting a workshop.

Which vehicles does it support?

The product messaging now positions the app for broad vehicle use, while the in-app workflow and support materials can guide users on compatibility details where needed.
