How-to advice example

Ask for targeted repair guidance, then work through the answer.

This example follows one complete advice flow from the original request screen through likely causes and the guided troubleshooting steps.

Step 1 Enter what you need help with

Start the how-to advice flow by describing the stuck starter motor bolt and attaching the supporting photo.

Step 2 Review advice

Review the first likely-cause advice for access angle, socket choice, and a bolt that may be seized or thread-locked.

Step 3 Follow the steps

Open the troubleshooting steps list and review the recommended removal approach before asking for more detail.

Step 4 Ask followup quesitons

Use the follow-up field to ask a practical workshop question when you need more detail before attempting the next step.

Step 5 Get the answers you need

Read the follow-up answer in context so the tool recommendation and bolt-access advice are clearer before you continue.

Step 6 Follow the steps

Open the detailed step view to work through the recommended check, timing, expected outcome, and workshop notes.

OBD II integration - Reading Codes

Read codes, inspect a fault, ask AI for context, and clear stored codes.

This example shows the built-in OBD workflow from the diagnostic snapshot and stored DTC list through fault detail, AI explanation, and code clearing.

Step 1 Read fault codes

Open the OBD diagnostic view to see the live snapshot, VIN context, readiness state, and the stored DTC list in one place.

Step 2 Select fault

Tap into a specific code to open the fault detail view and review the code in the context of the active vehicle profile.

Step 3 Have AI explain the code

Use the explain action to get a quick AI summary with likely causes, suggested checks, and a practical safety note.

Step 4 Clear codes

When appropriate, send Mode 04 to clear stored DTCs and reset related readiness monitors through the in-app confirmation flow.

Diagnosis flow example

Choose the active car, submit the issue, and review the likely causes.

FAQ

Questions drivers ask before they trust the diagnosis.

Clear answers help position the product as serious, capable, and useful for both beginners and experienced enthusiasts.

How does the AI diagnosis work?

The app analyses the problem description, available vehicle context, and supporting evidence to rank likely causes and recommend sensible next checks.

Can it use photos?

Yes. Photos can add context such as warning lights, leaks, damaged parts, labels, or VIN information that would otherwise be hard to describe accurately.

Does it work with OBD devices?

Pocket AI Mechanic is designed to work with compatible Bluetooth OBD hardware so code, VIN, and live data context can strengthen the diagnosis.

Is it suitable for beginners?

Yes. The workflow is built to make the next step clearer, whether you are an experienced enthusiast or just trying to understand a fault before visiting a workshop.

Which vehicles does it support?

We currently support over 370 different vehicles. If you don't see your car just let us know and we can add it!
